# Bridge Gap Note

This note explains what the bridge milestone closes from Week 6 and what still remains for the final console version.

## Gap Closed From Week 6

Week 6 is structurally useful, but it is not yet a fully usable console planner.

The bridge closes most of that behavioral gap by adding:

* real menu-driven interaction
* task entry through prompts
* task completion flow
* session logging flow
* readable task and session views
* progress summaries and simple recommendations
* persistent JSON data across runs

That makes this bridge version behaviorally much closer to the final app than Week 6 is.

## What Still Remains For The Final Version

The final console version still remains important because it is the polished behavioral reference.

It still adds or strengthens:

* more complete menu coverage
* task removal flow
* explicit reload behavior
* fuller refinement of summary and recommendation output
* final wording and coherence polish

## Instructional Value

This bridge matters because it makes the curriculum sequence more honest.

It shows that the final console version does not simply appear after Week 6 without an integration step.

Instead, StudyPilot becomes behaviorally fuller here, and then the final version serves as the polished reference point.
